About Varendra Museum

Varendra Museum is a historic museum, premier research centre, and popular visitor attraction situated at the heart of Rajshahi city. Maintained proudly by the University of Rajshahi, it holds a prestigious status as the very oldest public museum in Bangladesh.


Established in 1910, it was the first museum built in the region of East Bengal. The institution began its journey as the primary collection depository for the Varendra Research Society (Varendra Anushandhan Samiti) before receiving its formal museum identity. The historic name "Varendra" references an ancient geographical region encompassing modern northern Bangladesh. Influential landowners and dignitaries of the area, notably Prince Sharat Kumar Ray, contributed enormously by donating invaluable personal collections of historic antiquities to ensure its foundation.
